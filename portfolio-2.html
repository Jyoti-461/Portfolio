RECEIVE MESSAGES FOR FREE

Want to get notified instantly when someone contacts you? Follow these steps to set up free email/SMS alerts — no paid service required.

  • 1
    Use Formspree (Free Email Alerts) Go to formspree.io, create a free account, and link your form to receive all submissions directly to your Gmail/inbox. Free tier: 50 submissions/month.
  • 2
    SMS via Telegram Bot (Free) Create a Telegram Bot using @BotFather. Use the free Telegram Bot API to forward form submissions as instant Telegram messages — works like an SMS on your phone.
  • 3
    Alternative: Web3Forms (Free) Sign up at web3forms.com — get a free API key and receive unlimited email notifications without any backend code. Just paste the key in your HTML form.
  • 4
    Email-to-SMS via Gmail Most carriers support email-to-text. For example, number@txt.att.net (AT&T) or number@vtext.com (Verizon). Set a Gmail filter to forward contact emails there as free SMS.
